Pancakes

Description

Easy breakfast dish

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. Sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a large bowl
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter
  3. Mix until smooth
  4. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium-high heat
  5. Pour in the batter, about 1/4 cup for each pancake, and brown on both sides
